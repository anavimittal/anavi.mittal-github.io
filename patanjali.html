Patanjali Identity

The Institute of Patanjali Yog Peeth promises a treatment for body, soul and mind-
through Yoga, for fitness; Ayurveda, for herbal medication; and Prayer, for peace of mind.

Patanjali is an Indian FMCG born out of Haridwar and based on the concepts of the ancient wisdom of Yoga and Ayurveda. The initiative was highly appreciated when it was launched by Baba Ramdev in 2006, amassing many followers.

Patanjali brand is a staple found in every average Indian household. Simple, raw and green, the appeal is quite different from a brand built around theism. The chosen colours include the colour of mati (soil) and sap green (leaves). There is an undertone of saffron (kesari) to hint at the devotion.
