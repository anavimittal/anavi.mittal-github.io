About Me

Anavi
Pronunciation: (aan-uh-vi)
Meaning: Humane, Pure Origin: Sanskrit

I have been working as a UX Designer at CARS24 since completing my Bachelors of Design from NIFT New Delhi in June 2019.

Previously, I have interned at companies like Paytm, Ogilvy, Tupperware, and Times of India for UX Research, Advertising, Graphics, and Journalism- only to find solace in designing interactions.

When I am not reading or moving pixels, you can find me playing chess or flute. I can't speak much for those, but I have gotten better at creating edibles during the lockdown.

When I am feeling audaciously articulate, I pen down or sketch a few lines.
